The Detective Finding: Data Leakage
The extraordinary 97.33% accuracy hides an important discovery. Two features — boat_present and body_present — represent "future knowledge": whether a passenger boarded a lifeboat or had their body later recovered. Including this in a predictive model is like solving a mystery after reading the final chapter. These features don't predict survival — they confirm it. Removing them would give a more honest reflection of predictive power.

FEATURE 01
Title Extraction
Pulled titles (Mr, Miss, Mrs, Master…) from passenger names to encode social rank and age-group signals.
FEATURE 02
Family Size
Combined sibsp + parch to measure total family aboard — small families tended to fare better.
FEATURE 03
IsAlone
A binary flag for solo travellers — those travelling alone had notably lower survival odds.
IMPORTANT NOTE
Leakage Features
boat_present & body_present are post-event indicators — powerful but methodologically problematic.
